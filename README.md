## Introduction

**nf-pediatric** is an end-to-end connectomics pipeline for pediatric (0-18y) dMRI and sMRI brain scans. It performs preprocessing, tractography, t1 reconstruction, cortical and subcortical segmentation, and connectomics.

![nf-pediatric-schema](/assets/nf-pediatric-schema.png)

## Documentation

If you want to use `nf-pediatric`, head over to our documentation: https://scilus.github.io/nf-pediatric/ !

You will find detailed instructions on how to organize your inputs, launch the pipeline, and analyze your processed results. 

**If you encounter bugs or issues, feel free to open an issue!**
